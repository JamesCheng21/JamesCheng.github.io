I am a research fellow at National University of Singapore, working in the the Lab of Integrated Data, Energy Analysis + Simulation (IDEAS), led by Dr. Adrian Chong. My work is centered around buildings, where we spend over 90% of our daily lives. Pursuing a more intelligent and energy-efficient built environment, my research interest lies in the intersection of building science and cutting-edge computational technology, including machine learning, physics-based simulation, and optimization.
